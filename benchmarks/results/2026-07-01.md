## Key Finding (First Pass)

Across the five gold documents, **LexVaultMD delivers competitive accuracy at approximately 12× lower CPU time per page and 10× lower peak memory than Marker, and approximately 17× lower CPU time and 15× lower memory than Docling** — all at equal or superior automated heading and body fidelity scores on born-digital legal documents (contract, pleading, SEC 10-K).

LexVaultMD loses on two known-weakness documents in the `--ocr off` configuration:
- **deposition-sample.pdf** — left-margin line numbers are a confirmed failure mode shared by all extractors.
- **scanned-mixed-sample.pdf** — page 1 is a scanned image; `--ocr off` produces a placeholder. Gap closes once the WHOLE_PAGE_OCR renderer is complete.

Docling's table transformer produces better cross-page financial table reconstruction on the SEC 10-K — a document type where its compute premium partially justifies itself for firms running isolated document conversion jobs rather than high-throughput batch pipelines.
